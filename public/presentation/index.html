Copyright

Copyrights protects you original ideas from others access. While Copyleft allows you to make changes to other ideas and give them back

Copyleft

Is the name given to the copyright type that, unlike ordinary copyrights, provides the original right to work that they can change and distribute to people as they wish, provided that they are protected in the modified copy of the same right. Copyleft software licenses are considered protective and reciprocity according to liberal free software licenses.

Copyleft purpose

Copyleft demands future users of works are afforded the same freedoms to modify or manipulate an original work. Once someone has a copyleft license they are free to manipulate and develop work. Future users are then afforded the same rights

Whos uses copyleft?

The concept of copyleft is central to many programming projects, and the license is most commonly used for software, digital art, writings, and other creative content.

disadvantages

  • They impose strict conditions and obligations on anyone who modifies or distributes the software
  • It can limit the flexibility and creativity of the developers.
